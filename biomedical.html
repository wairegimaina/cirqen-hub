Core Solutions

1

Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS)

A centralized platform for managing biomedical assets throughout their lifecycle.

Key Capabilities

  • ✓ Asset registration and classification (imaging, lab, ICU, radiotherapy, etc.)
  • ✓ Preventive & corrective maintenance scheduling
  • ✓ Automated work orders and technician assignment
  • ✓ Spare parts and inventory tracking
  • ✓ Downtime analysis and failure trend monitoring
  • ✓ Compliance-ready maintenance records (ISO, IEC, WHO, hospital audits)
Impact

• Reduced equipment downtime
• Improved technician efficiency
• Full traceability for audits and accreditation

2

Calibration & Quality Assurance System

A digital calibration management system designed for precision-critical medical devices.

Key Capabilities

  • ✓ Calibration scheduling and reminders
  • ✓ Digital calibration certificates and traceability
  • ✓ Measurement uncertainty tracking
  • ✓ Instrument reference hierarchy (master → working standards)
  • ✓ Compliance with ISO 17025 and hospital QA requirements
Impact

• Accurate diagnostics and treatments
• Reduced clinical risk
• Strong regulatory compliance
